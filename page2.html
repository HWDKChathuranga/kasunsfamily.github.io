My Mother: The Radiant Homemaker

In the center of our family, there is a shining sun that is a woman whose loving warmth and contagious laughter make our lives better. Mother is the creator of this haven and she weaves magic in the normality. A mild hand creates warmth at home where beautiful smells linger on air and walls resound with joyous sounds. She never tires out in ensuring that we are all right and she does this at any cost placing our interests first. She is our North star pointing us on the right path, steady foundation against which we lean and the smiling ray of sunshine warming us each day.
